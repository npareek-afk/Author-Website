Journey Through AI Land

Author | Ph.D. Scholar | IT Professional

In a world rapidly transformed by algorithms, Journey Through AI Land serves as both a guide and a narrative exploration. This book demystifies complex AI concepts through engaging storytelling, making the future of technology accessible to everyone. From the fundamentals of machine learning to the ethical frontiers of artificial intelligence, this journey is designed for readers who want to understand the "how" and "why" of our digital evolution.
