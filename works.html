Journey Through AI Land

A friendly way to understand AI with ease.

A lot of people wish that someone could explain AI in a simple, friendly way, without the tech overwhelm. This book is a story-led introduction to the core building blocks of AI, written for anyone who wants clarity without complexity.

Join Kishi, your virtual travel buddy, as we explore Data, Learning, LLMs, Prompts, and Agentic AI through relatable stories and simple analogies.

  • How AI learns patterns and improves like humans
  • What powers tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and DALL-E
  • Why prompts matter — and how to write better ones
  • Why Agentic AI is the next leap after Generative AI
