Journey Through AI Land

A friendly introduction to the building blocks of AI

A lot of people wish that someone could explain AI in a simple, friendly way, without the tech overwhelm. “Journey Through AI Land” is a story-led introduction written for anyone who wants clarity without complexity.

No jargons. No intimidating tech talk. Just relatable stories and simple analogies with real-life examples. Accompanied by your virtual travel buddy Kishi, you'll discover how AI thinks, learns, and why it still needs humans.

  • ✔ How AI learns patterns like humans
  • ✔ What powers ChatGPT, Gemini, and other AI tools
  • ✔ Why prompts matter, and how to write better ones
  • ✔ The leap from Generative to Agentic AI
