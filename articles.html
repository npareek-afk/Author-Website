On the Horizon (Upcoming Book)

Journey Through AI Land 2: Building the Intelligent World

Currently in Progress

Artificial Intelligence is changing in visible and practical ways. What began as systems that responded to prompts will be examined as systems that can plan tasks, use tools, retrieve information, remember context, and act across multiple steps.

The ideas unfold in a story-led style, making complex systems a practical reference for the years ahead. This book will explore how intelligence has evolved from pattern recognition to structured reasoning.
